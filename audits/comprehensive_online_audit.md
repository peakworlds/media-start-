## 3. Trust & Authority Signals
*   **Third-Party Validation**: The core differentiator is independent HPLC & MS testing. However, with only one (1-star) public Trustpilot review, the external social proof breaks down, making internal "4.8-star" claims look fabricated. This is a critical gap.
*   **Action Required**: Launch a massive initiative to drive existing, satisfied customers from the Telegram group to leave 5-star reviews on external platforms like Trustpilot, using product/store credit as the lever.

---

## 4. The Funnel Disconnect
The current funnel drives users from private communities to a website that doesn't fully embrace the brand's aesthetic potential. It relies purely on the urgency of "12-hour dispatch" and "99% purity." A comprehensive shift to a stark, cyberpunk "Shadow Lab" aesthetic will make the *experience* of buying as premium as the product claims to be.
