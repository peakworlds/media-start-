# Audit: Aesthetic Deep Dive
## Peak Haven Australia

---

## 1. Multi-Sensory Brand Identity

### A. Extended Color System
| Color Type | Hex Code | Purpose |
|------------|----------|---------|
| Primary | `#0B0C10` | The void / Black. Representing the sterile environment. |
| Secondary | `#1F2833` | Gunmetal grey for sub-headers and borders. |
| Tertiary | `#C5C6C7` | Sterile silver for readable body text on black. |
| Accent 1 | `#66FCF1` | Neon cyan for CTAs and critical data points. |
| Accent 2 | `#45A29E` | Deep teal for secondary buttons and states. |

### B. Texture & Material Language
- **Matte Black Carbon**: Packaging materials and unboxing experience.
- **Frosted Glass / Condensation**: Visuals emphasizing the cold-chain shipping logistics.
- **Brushed Steel/Aluminum**: Interface components in digital deliverables.
- **Dry Ice Vapor**: Used subtly in background imagery or video transitions.

### C. Audio/Sonic Identity
- **Vibe**: Low-frequency analog synths and clinical, metallic precision clicks (e.g. *Blade Runner 2049* Wallace Corp themes).
- **Soundscape**: In any video marketing, a low electrical hum beneath the music, followed by a sterile 'vault door closing' or 'glass tink' sound effect as the brand tag.

### D. Motion & Animation
- **Web Interactions**: Ultra-fast, frictionless snap-to-place animations mimicking high-end lab robotics. No bouncy or playful curves; strictly ease-in-out linear movements.
- **Video Style**: Slow-motion macro-photography of vials rotating perfectly on axis. Data streams (HPLC purity graphs) overlaid with digital scrambling visual effects.

---

## 3. Brand Twin: Pop Culture Triangulation
- **Environment**: Tyrell Corporation / Wallace Corp Laboratories (*Blade Runner*).
- **Character Archetype**: The detached, brilliant, morally-neutral scientist providing the tools for human evolution (e.g. Dr. Robert Ford in *Westworld*).
- **Era**: 2035 Near-Future Corporate Biotech.

---

## 4. The Execution Gap
**Current State**: Visually stuck in a generic medical/tech blue-and-white phase.
**Required Shift**: A full pivot into dark mode. By shifting to Onyx, Silver, and Cyan, Peak Haven stops looking like a generic medical supplier and starts looking like an exclusive, bleeding-edge "Shadow Lab" pushing human research forward. This justifies the premium pricing and offsets the anxiety of strict "no returns" policies by overwhelming the user with authority.
