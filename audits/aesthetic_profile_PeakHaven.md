# Aesthetic Profile: Peak Haven Australia

> **Vibe Check**: Clinical, Premium, Shadow-Tech, High-Velocity, Uncompromising.

---

## Visual Identity Matrix

### 1. Color Palette
| Color | Hex Code | Usage |
|-------|----------|-------|
| **Primary** | `#0B0C10` | Backgrounds, heavy branding (Onyx Black) |
| **Secondary** | `#1F2833` | Text, primary accents (Gunmetal) |
| **Accent** | `#66FCF1` | Buttons, callouts, energy (Neon Cyan) |
| **Neutral** | `#C5C6C7` | Balance, whitespace (Sterile Grey) |

### 2. Typography
* **Headings**: `Inter` (Sans-Serif) — *Why: Represents modern, stark clinical precision. It screams tech-enabled biology.*
* **Body**: `Roboto Mono` (Sans-Serif) — *Why: Evokes laboratory reports, chemical coding, and unvarnished data.*

### 3. Imagery Style
* **Lighting**: Harsh, high-contrast studio lighting or stark sterile medical environments. Shadows are deep to evoke the "grey market" edge.
* **Composition**: Symmetrical, microscopic close-ups of lyophilized pucks, glass vials, and condensation to emphasize the cold-chain logistics.
* **Textures**: Frosted glass, brushed aluminum, dry ice vapor, and matte black surfaces.
