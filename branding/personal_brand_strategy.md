# Personal Brand Strategy: Peak Haven Australia

> **NOTE**: The Founder operates under an active "Ghost Protocol" and deliberately retains zero public-facing profile.

## 1. Strategic Refusal
A personal brand would jeopardize the core corporate mechanism. If a name or face is attached, the risk profile of selling "research only" peptides scales exponentially. Therefore, this strategy focuses on cementing the *Institutional Voice* rather than a personal one.

## 2. Institutional Content Push
Instead of LinkedIn Thought Leadership, the entity will deploy "White Papers" on Telegram and the website blog.

### The 90-Day "Shadow" Sprint
*   **Month 1**: Publish the definitive 2,000-word guide to "Deciphering HPLC and Janoshik Reports" under the banner of the "Peak Haven Analytical Team."
*   **Month 2**: Release a detailed operational breakdown of the domestic cold-chain infrastructure to establish logistical dominance.
*   **Month 3**: Deploy technical Deep-Dives on new compound stacks (e.g., "The Pharmacokinetics of SLU-PP-332").

All content is authored by "Peak Haven Research & Support."
