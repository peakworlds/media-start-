# Brand Book: Peak Haven Australia

## 1. Brand Heart
*   **Mission**: To supply the Australian research community with the highest-purity, independently verified synthetic compounds available, dispatched with uncompromising speed.
*   **Vision**: To be the undisputed "Gold Standard" for biological integrity in the Southern Hemisphere.
*   **Values**: Absolute Transparency (Testing), Uncompromising Logistics (Cold-Chain), Clinical Authority (Language).

## 2. Voice & Tone
*   **Voice**: Clinical, detached, precise, and authoritative. Extraneous adjectives are removed. 
*   **Tone**: Unapologetic. "We do not compromise. We do not accept returns. We do not guess."
*   **Forbidden Words**: "Skinny", "Shred", "Swole", "Gain", "Medicine", "Cure", "Human Use".
*   **Required Phraseology**: "Phase Protocols", "Lyophilized Integrity", "Metabolic Agonist Arrays", "Third-Party Verified".

## 3. Visual Identity (The Shadow Lab)
*   **Colors**: Onyx Black (#0B0C10), Gunmetal Grey (#1F2833), Neon Cyan (#66FCF1), Sterile Grey (#C5C6C7).
*   **Typography**: Inter (Headings) + Roboto Mono (Body/Data).
*   **Imagery**: Ultra-macro, sterile environments. Harsh contrast. No smiling lifestyle models.

## 4. Competitive Differentiation
Peak Haven looks and behaves like a multi-million dollar defense contractor or leading biotech lab, while competitors look like protein-powder dropshippers. The 10x Purity Guarantee makes all other suppliers look fraudulent by comparison.
