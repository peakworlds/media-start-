# Aesthetic Strategy: Peak Haven Australia

## 1. The Art Direction Manifesto
Peak Haven is a "Shadow Lab." Fast, ruthless, clinical. Visuals should evoke a visceral sense of cutting-edge technology and molecular reality.

## 2. Photography & Videography Briefs
### Photography (Product)
*   *Subject*: Macro shots of the lyophilized puck inside the vial. Let the user see the "crystal clear" quality of the synthesis.
*   *Lighting*: Hard side-lighting on a matte black surface.
*   *Props*: Dry ice vapor, sterile surgical trays, HPLC readouts in the background with shallow depth of field.

### Videography (Logistics Focus)
*   *Subject*: The 12-hour dispatch promise.
*   *Execution*: High-speed, kinetic tracking shots of orders being packed. The sound of a thermal seal zipping shut. A crisp label printed and slapped on the box. 
*   *Vibe*: Visceral efficiency.
