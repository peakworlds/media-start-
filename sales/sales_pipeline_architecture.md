# Sales Pipeline Architecture: Peak Haven Australia

## 1. Funnel Overarching Structure
*   **Awareness**: Dark-Social Seeding (Telegram/Discord proofs).
*   **Acquisition**: The "10x Purity Guarantee" & the HPLC verification documents on the product page.
*   **Activation**: First single vial purchase (diagnostic run).
*   **Revenue**: The repeat-purchase "Phase Protocol" (multi-vial stack).
*   **Referral**: Inner Haven store credit for 3rd-party validation (Trustpilot/Reddit).

## 2. Lead Scoring (E-com Equivalent)
*   **VIP (> $500 monthly)**: Direct SMS / Telegram integration for absolute supply assurance. Automatically prioritized for "sold out" stock notifications.
*   **Core ($150 - $499 monthly)**: Sent emails regarding new protocol configurations.
*   **Churn Risk**: Has not reordered beyond a 3-month gap (indicating protocol failure or defection to a cheaper source). Trigger automated re-engagement via VIP "Free Cold Shipping" test.
