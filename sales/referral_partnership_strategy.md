## 1. The Core Objective

- **Who they are:** Independent Australian health/fitness podcasters with 5k-20k highly engaged, trust-based listeners. They discuss esoteric longevity, cold plunging, and nootropics.
- **Why them:** Their audience already understands the science. They don't need "What is BPC-157?", they need "Where do I get 99% pure BPC-157 in Australia without it melting in transit?"
- **The Value Prop (For Them):** Pure access. Free, verified, high-end "Phase Arrays" worth over $800 to test, plus a permanent 15% revenue share for their audience code.

### Profile B: The Private Clinic / Coach (The Gatekeeper)
- **Who they are:** Elite strength/conditioning coaches or TRT (Testosterone) clinic consultants who are structurally prevented from selling peptides directly due to liability, but whose clients demand it.
- **Why them:** They have a captive audience of 50-100 high-net-worth individuals spending $5,000/year on optimization.
- **The Value Prop (For Them):** A reliable, risk-free supplier for their clients that doesn't sell bunk Chinese powder. They receive a discrete 20% kickback paid in crypto/USDC for every client referred via dedicated Telegram links.
