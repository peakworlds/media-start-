## PRE-ROLL PRODUCTION NOTES

- **Voice:** Use ElevenLabs "Callum" voice (deep, authoritative, UK-adjacent neutral) or a contracted voice actor. Do not use a friendly, warm tone. This script is delivered with the measured calm of a briefing.
- **Visual Style:** Onyx Black (`#0B0C10`) backgrounds. Harsh, single-overhead surgical LED lighting. Neon Cyan (`#66FCF1`) text overlays. Macro shots only — no wide product shots.
- **Music:** Ambient, dark synth-wave. No beats. Sterile silence between emphasis points is a valid production choice.
