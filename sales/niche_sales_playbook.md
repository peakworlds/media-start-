# Niche Sales Playbook: Peak Haven Australia

## 1. The Sales Environment
Because Peak Haven operates a direct-to-consumer E-Commerce model in a legally grey industry, traditional "sales calls" or aggressive outbound SDR campaigns are impossible. Sales happen through establishing an impenetrable visual and structural *Trust Matrix*.
The CRM stages apply to E-Com funnels rather than specific human interactions.

## 2. E-Commerce Pipeline "Sales" Stages
1. **Cold Lead (Awareness):** Follows Twitter/Reddit biohacker threads or IG tags.
2. **Discovery (Website Ping):** Clicks the Linktree. Analyzes the layout geometry and dark-mode aesthetic for professionalism.
3. **Assessment (The Proof Check):** The user scrolls the product page seeking Janoshik/HPLC PDFs and looking for the "12-Hour Cold Chain" promise.
4. **Objection Block (Checkout Friction):** Encounters the "No Returns" policy and the "Research Only" waiver. Overcomes immediately via the "10x Purity Guarantee."
5. **Closed Won (The Delivery):** The logistics (arriving with dry ice/thermopads intact) constitute the first "Sales Call" delivering on the promise.

## 3. Handling Niche Objections "At Checkout"
*   **Objection 1**: "Is this actually 99% pure or is it a generic Chinese import with a slapped label?"
    *   **The Close**: Embedded 3rd party testing documentation directly in the image carousel. You don't have to trust us, trust the lab.
*   **Objection 2**: "Is this going to be degraded sitting in a post office truck in 35-degree heat for 3 days?"
    *   **The Close**: 12-Hour Cold-Chain Dispatch. "We do not accept returns specifically because we cannot guarantee the cold chain once it leaves our facility. Your order arrives exactly as it was synthesized."

## 4. Upselling The Protocols
*   Instead of "Buy 2 get 1 Free", the upsell path is "Phase I Protocol (Healing)" combining BPC-157, TB-500, and complimentary reconstitution kits. Add the BAC water directly into the cart bump to remove the "how do I use this?" friction entirely.
