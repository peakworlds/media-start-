## 3. The Pivot Protocol (Trajectory Management)

*(What to do if the operation fails.)*

### Scenario 1: TGA / Stripe Shutdown (The "Kill/Keep" Matrix)
*   **Trigger:** If Stripe flags the account for "Unapproved Pharmaceuticals" or the website is shadowbanned by host platforms.
*   **Keep:** The Inner Haven Telegram Syndicate (the actual customer list), the supplier relationships, the Janoshik testing protocol.
*   **Kill:** The public Shopify endpoint.
*   **Pivot (Transform):** Transition entirely into a private Crypto-only, Discord/Telegram-based syndicate. "The Vault" becomes the entire operating model.

### Scenario 2: Wholesale Market Crash / Undercutting
*   **Trigger:** Dropshippers flood the market with heavily discounted, high-purity inventory, eroding the 40% premium.
*   **Keep:** The cold-chain logistics brand and customer LTV.
*   **Kill:** The "Chemical Supplier" positioning.
*   **Pivot (Transform):** Pivot the company into a high-ticket "White Glove" protocol design service. Transition from selling the *tools* to selling the $5k *map* (customized, medically-monitored bloodwork/optimization plans utilizing our own raw materials).
