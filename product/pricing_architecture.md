# Pricing & Offer Architecture

**Company:** Peak Haven Australia
**Date:** 2026-03-06

---

## 1. The Strategy (The Frame)
The grey-market biohacking space consists of three types of pricing:
1. **The Telehealth/Clinic Extortion:** Massive markups (+1000%) simply for writing a Schedule 4 prescription to access mainstream compounding pharmacies.
2. **The Dropship Bottom-Feeder:** Selling unverified powder below $50 per vial. Zero trust, high seizure rate.
3. **The Shadow Lab (Peak Haven):** The sweet spot. Operating structurally as a high-margin E-Com offering verifiably pure chemicals without the "Clinic" markup, but with the aesthetic and logistical rigor of a tier-1 operation. We charge a 30-40% premium *over* the dropshippers, solely to fund the cold-chain logistics and the 10x Janoshik Guarantee.

---

## 2. The Core Value Ladder

### Tier 1: The Diagnostics (Single Vials)
**(The Gateway and Purity Test for new users.)**
- **Price Point:** $85 - $195 (Varies wildly by molecule—e.g., TB-500 is cheap, Retatrutide is highly expensive).
- **The Psychological Hook:** "Verify our standard before you run a full array."
- **Scope Limitations:** Express thermal shipping eats into single vial margins severely. Must actively upsell reconstitution kits (water + supplies) for +$35.
- **The True Purpose:** To capture their email and Telegram ID for future LTV, not immediate profit.

### Tier 2: The Phase Protocols / Arrays (Main Offer)
**(The primary marketing focus. Bundling synergistic peptides to treat biological pathways entirely.)**
- **Price Point:** $450 - $1,100
- **Examples:**
  - **Phase I: Structural Integrity Array:** (BPC-157 [2x], TB-500 [2x], GHK-Cu, Reconstitution Kit).
  - **Phase II: Metabolic Agonist Array:** (Tirzepatide/Retatrutide staggered dosing schedule, Syringes, 10x Guarantee).
- **The Reframing (Why buy this?):** "Buying loose vials requires you to build the map yourself. The Phase Protocols deliver the exact map and the exact chemical codes simultaneously in one cold-chain box."
- **Economics:** High gross margin ($300-$800 profit per array).

### Tier 3: The Vault Drops / Subscriptions
**(Recurring LTV through scarcity.)**
- **Price Point:** $1,500 - $3,000 / Quarter
- **The Hook:** Elite biohackers commit to 3-month supply cycles. "The Syndicate Reserve."
- **The Execution:** Only available inside the encrypted Inner Haven Telegram. Subscribers get front-of-line access to Janoshik-verified batches before they hit the public storefront (mitigates global raw-powder supply chain anxiety).

## 3. Objection Handling & Pricing Reframes

| The Objection | The Tactical Reframe | The Underlying Math |
|---------------|----------------------|---------------------|
| "I can get BPC-157 from [Dropshipper X] for $50." | "We don't sell $50 powder because we spend $50 on Janoshik testing and -20°C logistics to ensure you don't inject degraded garbage. They rely on ambient shipping." | Quality costs money. If it's too cheap, it's fake. |
| "A clinic charges me $800 just for the prescription." | "We supply the exact identical analytical-grade scaffolds direct to researchers. You are paying for the chemical, not a 15-minute Zoom call." | You skip the 'Doctor' tax. |
| "Why am I forced to buy a Reconstitution Kit?" | "Because tap water destroys the peptide structure. We will not sell you 99.8% pure chemicals if you destroy them during mixing." | Framing the upsell as an uncompromising defense of quality. |

---

## 4. The Kill Criteria
*   **AOV < $120:** If Average Order Value stays below $120, express thermal shipping costs will suffocate the business. Price increases or mandatory minimums must be instituted.
*   **Stripe Velocity Flags:** If growth (>50% MoM) triggers a Stripe audit due to irregular volumes, the store must intentionally gate access via passwords sent only to the Telegram channel to manage financial risk.
