# Product-Market Fit (PMF) Validation Framework

**Company:** Peak Haven Australia
**Offer to Validate:** Concept of "Phase Protocols" (Synergistic biological arrays instead of single diagnostic vials)
**Date:** 2026-03-06

---

## 1. Validation Hypothesis
**The Hypothesis:** High-net-worth biohackers in Australia will happily pay a 40% premium over market rate if they can acquire complete, synergistically mapped "Phase Protocols" (e.g., BPC-157 + TB-500 + BAC Water Kits) bundled for specific biological outcomes rather than researching and sourcing individual compounds.

## 2. Testing Constraints & Rules
- **No Paid Ads:** All validation must happen via Dark Social outreach (Telegram, Discord, Reddit DMs).
- **The "Skin-in-the-Game" Rule:** Validation is NOT a survey. Validation is a credit card swipe. A "yes" means nothing unless they transfer funds.

## 3. The Discovery Interview Script (Dark Social DMs)
**The Frame:** Peer-to-peer intelligence gathering inside a Telegram syndicate.

**The Script:**
> "Hey [Name], noticed you dropping insights on the TB-500 thread. We're beta-testing a closed-loop cold-chain array here in Aus. Basically mapping synergistic compounds (like BPC + TB) into single 60-day protocol kits, verified by Janoshik, shipped domestic in 12 hours. The math means it's about $450 per phase, but zero customs risk and zero heat degradation. Is that a protocol structure you'd deploy, or do you prefer sourcing single vials internationally to save cash?"

## 4. Evaluation Matrix & Thresholds

| Response | Classification | Next Action |
|----------|----------------|-------------|
| "I build my own stacks to save money." | **Disqualified** (Price Shopper) | Log as rejection. Do not pursue. |
| "What's the dosage protocol?" | **Curious** | Shift to clinical details. Provide the array specs. |
| "Send the test PDFs and the Stripe link." | **Validated Buy** | Send payment link. Provide priority fulfillment. |

**The PMF Threshold:**
- **Target:** 50 DMs sent to qualified biohackers.
- **Fail:** < 3 purchases (Offer is too expensive or trust is too low).
- **Pivot:** 3-5 purchases (Needs refinement on pricing or stack configuration).
- **Pass (PMF Reached):** > 5 purchases at full margin. Proceed to scale the Phase Protocols across the entire store.
