4.  **Stock Scarcity / Synthesis Delays**: (Supply). Retatrutide/Tirzepatide shortages globally. *Mitigation*: Heavy inventory buffering during off-peak windows.
5.  **Community De-Platforming**: (Social). Subreddits or Discord channels getting nuked. *Mitigation*: Own the email list. Over-communicate via Telegram which resists platform bans.
