## Section A: Trust Level Definitions

*To successfully sell $800+ biological arrays in an unregulated, high-risk grey market, the buyer evaluates trust linearly. We must build from the ground up while maintaining the founder's anonymity.*

### Level 1 — Institutional Surface Credibility (The "Who is this?")
- **Current State Score:** 4/10
- **The Gap:** The website mixes premium pricing with cheap "Summer Shred" fitness language, making it look like a drop-shipping scam, not a clinical laboratory.
- **Action Required:** Execute the "Shadow Lab" aesthetic (Onyx/Cyan). Remove all human models. Replace all gym vernacular with dense, uncompromising scientific terminology. The institution must appear ruthless, precise, and flawlessly executed.

### Level 2 — Proof of Work (The "Can they actually do it?")
- **Current State Score:** 2/10
- **The Gap:** The brand promises purity but hides the actual testing data on a separate "Verification" tab, which no one reads.
- **Action Required:** Embed independent Janoshik mass spectrometry (HPLC) PDF scans directly into the image carousel of every single product. Make the proof unavoidable.

### Level 3 — Social Validation (The "Who else says they can do it?")
- **Current State Score:** 3/10
- **The Gap:** The Trustpilot score is sitting at 3.2. In the grey market, a sub-4.5 score means you are selling bunk powder or stealing money.
- **Action Required:** Activate the "Inner Haven" Telegram syndicate. Offer a $50 store credit bounty to the first 100 verified purchasers who leave an honest, detailed 5-star review on Trustpilot mentioning the "12-hour dispatch."

### Level 4 — Operational Credibility (The "Are they a real business?")
- **Current State Score:** 7/10
- **The Gap:** You operate through an Australian registered Trust (The Trustee for The Peak Haven Trust) and a Tier-1 Stripe account. This is excellent. However, this structure is invisible to the user.
- **Action Required:** Publish a hyper-detailed "Cold-Chain Logistics" page. Show photos of the thermal bags, the dry ice, and the express post manifesting process. Prove the physical infrastructure exists.

### Level 5 — Risk Reversal (The "What if it fails?")
- **Current State Score:** 1/10
- **The Gap:** A strict "No Returns" policy at checkout creates massive purchase anxiety. It sounds like an evasion tactic.
- **Action Required:** Deploy the **10x Purity Contract**. "If an independent, accredited lab test shows our vial is <99% pure, we will refund you 10x the purchase price. We do not accept returns on dispatched vials because we refuse to restock items that have left our -20°C temperature controls. Our strictness guarantees your safety."
