# Strategic KPI Calendar: Peak Haven Australia

## Month 1: The Transition
*   **Primary KPI**: Trustpilot Velocity.
*   **Target**: 15 Verified 5-Star Reviews.
*   **ROI Measure**: Cart abandonment rates dropping by 10% due to visible trust markers on checkout.

## Month 2: The Telegram Launch
*   **Primary KPI**: Inner Haven Headcount.
*   **Target**: Convert 30% of historical email purchasers into the active Telegram community.
*   **ROI Measure**: Cost of Acquisition dropping down to zero for repeat purchases directly linked from Telegram drops.

## Month 3: Traffic Quality Shift
*   **Primary KPI**: Phase Protocol AOV (Average Order Value).
*   **Target**: Shift overall AOV from standard $90 to >$200.
*   **ROI Measure**: Ensuring cold-chain express shipping costs are fully absorbed by the high margins on bundled orders rather than single diagnostic vials.

## Month 4-12: The Flywheel
*   **Primary KPI**: Cohort Retention Rate (3-Month Re-order metric).
*   **Target**: 65% of protocol users executing a second purchase.
*   **ROI Measure**: LTV / CAC ratio exceeding 5:1 based on pure organic/dark-social traffic with zero paid ad spend required.
