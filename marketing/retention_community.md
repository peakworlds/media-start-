# Retention & Community Manual: Peak Haven Australia

## 1. The LTV Engine: "Inner Haven" Telegram
*   **The Problem**: Customers buy a vial, use it, and forget to restock, or seek cheaper alternatives because there is zero brand loyalty to standard medical dropshippers.
*   **The Solution**: A gated, invite-only Telegram commmunity. Every order receives a foiled QR card. 
*   **The Vibe**: An underground cabal of researchers sharing their most successful protocols. Administered by "Peak Haven Support" with clinical detachment. Strict rules against "human use" language (enforced by bots kicking users).

## 2. Retention Incentivization
*   **Protocol Refills**: Reordering a full Phase Array gives the user priority in stock-shortage windows (particularly relevant for new generation GLP-1/Agonist molecules).
*   **The Third-Party Loop**: Reward members who commission their own independent HPLC tests. If a customer pays Janoshik to test a Peak Haven vial and posts it in the group, they get refunded the test cost + $500 in store credit. *This creates an unassailable fortress of user-generated proof.*

## 3. Disincentivizing Churn
By holding the standard of 12-hour cold chain shipping, anyone who leaves for a cheaper competitor will immediately notice the difference when the competitor's package shows up 4 days late, warm to the touch. The product itself, via logistics, becomes the ultimate retention mechanism.
