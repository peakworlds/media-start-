# Paid Advertising Plan: Peak Haven Australia

## Executive Summary: DON'T DO IT
Due to the TGA classifications of research peptides and the algorithmic sensitivity of Meta, TikTok, and Google Ads toward "illicit healthcare products," standard paid media is a massive risk. Pushing ads for "BPC-157" or "Retatrutide" will immediately trigger business manager bans, potentially blacklisting the domain.

## The Alternative "Paid" Strategy

### 1. Influencer / Affiliate Seeding
The entire ad budget should be re-allocated into supplying high-level Australian biohackers, strength coaches, and longevity podcasters with free cold-chain supply. The ROI on a $400 protocol stack sent to a person with an engaged Discord/Telegram community vastly outperforms $400 in penalized Meta spend.

### 2. Search Intent Capture (SEO instead of SEM)
Instead of paying for clicks on Google via SEM (which is subject to policy shutdowns), dominate the organic search for "Janoshik verified Australian peptide supplier."

### 3. Dark Social Bounties
Paying out "bounties" (massive store credits) to existing customers who publicly verify the shipping speed and purity testing on platforms like Reddit or Trustpilot.
