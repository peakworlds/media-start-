# Market Trends Analysis: Peak Haven Australia

## 1. Macro Trends
- **The Decentralization of Health**: Patients are self-diagnosing and bypassing traditional GPs for optimization.
- **The GLP-1 & Agonist Boom**: Obesity and metabolic drugs have normalized synthetic compounds in the mainstream.
- **Supply Chain Reshoring**: Consumers demand domestic verification after years of unreliable overseas shipping.

## 2. Micro Trends
- **Testing as Marketing**: Independent third-party testing (Janoshik style) is now the primary marketing mechanism in grey markets.
- **Dark Social Displacement**: Marketing has moved entirely off algorithmic platforms (Meta/Google) and into Discord/Telegram for restriction-free discussion.

## 3. Category Design
Peak Haven does not sell "Fitness Supplements" nor "Prescription Drugs." They own the category of **High-Velocity Verified Chemical Scaffolding**. They are the AWS for biological researchers.
