# Org & Culture Playbook: Peak Haven Australia

> **Purpose**: Define the company's operating system for people, including culture, organizational architecture, hiring sequence, and employee lifecycle management inside a decentralized grey-market framework.

## 1. Culture & Core Values
> The behavioral operating system that dictates who gets hired, promoted, and fired. This is not a tech startup; this is a precision supply chain operation.

### The Peak Haven Core Values

1. **The Chain is Sacred (Uncompromising Integrity)**: 
   * **What it means**: Our entire business model collapses if a vial sits at room temperature for 48 hours. The cold-chain is our only moat.
   * **Behaviors we reward**: Delaying a dispatch because the thermal wrap seal was 95% perfect instead of 100%. Driving to the post office at 2 AM to make the express cutoff.
   * **Anti-behaviors we reject**: Saying "it's probably fine" when dealing with temperature tolerances or chemical integrity.

2. **Verify or Die (The Janoshik Standard)**: 
   * **What it means**: We don't trust our own suppliers. We don't trust our own marketing. We only trust third-party mass spectrometry. 
   * **Behaviors we reward**: Quarantining a $15,000 shipment of raw powder because the Janoshik assay showed 98% purity instead of 99%.
   * **Anti-behaviors we reject**: Making scientific or biological claims without linking directly to the underlying raw data.

3. **The Ghost Protocol (Operational Discretion)**: 
   * **What it means**: We operate in a highly scrutinized, legally complex environment. We do not seek personal fame. We do not use our faces in marketing. We are an institution.
   * **Behaviors we reward**: Communicating flawlessly via encrypted channels (Telegram). Removing metadata from uploaded files. Strict adherence to OpSec protocols.
   * **Anti-behaviors we reject**: Posting "behind the scenes" warehouse selfies on personal Instagram accounts.

### Operating Principles
* **Communication Baseline**: Signal for encrypted internal comms. Telegram for syndicate (customer) management. Zero use of standard SMS or Meta platforms for internal logistics.
* **Decision Making**: Decentralized Execution. If a node operator (dispatch tech) sees a compromised vial, they have unilateral authority to destroy it without asking the Founder.
* **Meeting Culture**: None. Asynchronous dispatch manifests only.

* **Philosophy**: We pay above market rate for warehouse/logistics work because this is not standard picking/packing. It requires medical-grade attention to detail and absolute discretion. We pay in fiat or crypto, based on contractor preference.
