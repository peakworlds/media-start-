## 3. High-Priority Risk Register & Business Continuity

### Risk 1: TGA Domain Takedown & Stripe Ban
**Description:** The Therapeutic Goods Administration (Australia) classifies all injectable peptides as Schedule 4 (Prescription Only). If Peak Haven is designated as an illegal pharmacy rather than a "Research Chemical Supplier", the domain will be shut down and merchant accounts frozen.
**Mitigation (The Firewall):**
1. Never use terms like "Human," "Heal," "Muscle," "Fat Loss," or "Injection."
2. Maintain the strictest "In-Vitro Analytical Use Only" disclaimers on every pixel of the site.
3. Keep $50k in stablecoins/crypto reserves off-exchange to rebuild the site offshore if necessary.
4. Funnel the customer database into the decentralized Telegram channel. The website is disposable; the customer list (The Syndicate) is the asset.

### Risk 2: Physical Inventory Seizure (Customs/ABF)
**Description:** Australian Border Force seizes a $20k shipment of raw Retatrutide powder from the synthesis lab, demanding import permits that do not exist for the grey market.
**Mitigation (Redundancy):**
1. Never order more than $10k worth of stock in a single package.
2. Maintain at least 6 weeks of inventory in physical deep freezers on Australian soil across two distinct geographic nodes (WA and East Coast).
3. If Node A is raided, Node B operates at 100% capacity to fulfill orders transparently while Node A is structurally rebuilt.

### Risk 3: The Cold-Chain Collapse (Summer Heatwaves)
**Description:** AusPost delays a package during a 42°C week in Queensland, melting the gel packs and degrading $800 worth of biologicals.
**Mitigation:**
1. Upgrade to VIP courier accounts with guaranteed express networking.
2. Trigger the "Immediate Replenish" protocol: If a package is flagged as delayed in the network beyond 72 hours, express ship a replacement instantly before the customer even asks. Claim the loss via corporate insurance later.
3. Switch off dispatch from Thursday mid-day to Sunday evening. Never allow highly-sensitive molecules to sit in a hot postal sorting facility over the weekend. All shipments must dispatch Mon-Wed.
