# Content Production SOP

**Company:** Peak Haven Australia
**Date:** 2026-03-06

---

## 1. The Content Philosophy (The "Shadow Lab" Strategy)

Peak Haven operates on the Ghost Protocol. We do not film our faces. We do not do "Hey guys, welcome back to my channel" vlogs. We do not post motivational fitness quotes.

The entire content strategy is built entirely on **Documentation of Proof** and **Education**.

If we film physical content, we film extreme macro-shots of vials, the blue-gloved hands picking the lyophilized pucks out of the deep freeze, the thermal packing manifesting process, and the literal mass spectrometry PDFs. The aesthetic is Onyx Black and Neon Cyan, scored by ambient cyberpunk synth or absolute silence. 

---

## 2. The Production Engine (Weekly Cadence)

### Monday: The Raw Material Shoot (Physical Node)
**Time Block:** 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM
1.  **Objective:** Batch-shoot all B-roll needed for the week during the live dispatch process (so it doesn't fake the timeframe).
2.  **Angle List (6 clips required):**
    - The -20°C freezer opening, dry ice vapor pouring out.
    - Close-up: Tweezers extracting a vial of BPC-157 (label perfectly in focus).
    - Macro: The vacuum-sealer crimping shut over the silver Mylar pouch.
    - Scanning the Janoshik verification QR code with a phone screen visible.
    - Taping the black shipping box shut.
    - Time-lapse of 20 boxes stacked next to the thermal sensors.
3.  **Gear:** iPhone 15 Pro on a tripod (Macro lens mode at 4k/60fps).
4.  **Lighting:** Pitch black room. Only illuminate the sorting table with a single harsh surgical LED light overhead to create dramatic shadows.

### Tuesday: Audio/Voice-Over Sourcing
**Time Block:** 2:00 PM - 3:00 PM
The Ghost Founder rarely uses their real voice.
1. Use ElevenLabs (AI Voice) or a contracted clinical voice actor to read the 3 required scripts for the week (e.g., from `video_scripts_month_1.md`).
2. Download ambient synth-wave audio backing tracks from Artlist or Epidemic Sound.

### Wednesday: The Assembly Line (Editing)
**Time Block:** 9:00 AM - 12:00 PM
1. Transfer 4K footage and AI voiceovers into Premiere Pro or CapCut.
2. Ensure color grading matches the Brand Book (Onyx shadows, cool Cyan highlights, lowered saturation on flesh tones on hands).
3. Overlay the exact Janoshik PDF readout onto the footage when the voiceover mentions "Purity."
4. Add aggressive, fast-paced hard cuts on the beat drops.
5. Export 3x vertical format reels (1080x1920) optimized for Instagram / Telegram Drops.

### Thursday: The Written Word (Copy & Email)
**Time Block:** 10:00 AM - 1:00 PM
1. Draft the 2 weekly Telegram Syndicate broadcasts. Total clinical tone. 
2. Draft 1 educational email for the Klaviyo autoresponder flow based on the "Reconstitution Math Protocol."
3. Synthesize the core "Case Study" (e.g., The 12-Hour Cold Chain Test) into a 600-word blog post for SEO indexing on Shopify.

### Friday: Scheduling & Distribution
1. Upload all Reels natively to Instagram as Drafts. 
2. Map out the Telegram Drops for Monday, Wednesday, and Friday spikes.
3. Schedule the educational email broadcast via Klaviyo for Sunday 8:00 PM (highest open rate for biohackers reading protocols for the week ahead).
