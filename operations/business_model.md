# Operations: Business Model Summary

**Company:** Peak Haven Australia
**Date:** 2026-03-06

---

## 1. The Core Engine
**Model:** Direct-to-Consumer (D2C) Premium E-Commerce via Dark Social.
**Product:** High-purity, Janoshik-verified biological scaffolds (Research Peptides).
**Pricing Strategy:** 40% premium above market dropshippers, justified by independent mass spectrometry and a strict 12-hour cold-chain dispatch SLA.

## 2. Supply Chain Architecture
*   **Synthesis (Offshore):** Raw lyophilized powder imported from tier-1 compounding facilities.
*   **Verification (The Moat):** Every batch is intercepted, blinded, and sent to Janoshik Analytical for HPLC purity testing. 
*   **Storage (Node Prime):** Cleared batches are held in commercial -20°C deep freezers at decentralized, secure Australian locations (WA / East Coast).
*   **Packaging:** Polyurethane thermal mailers, dry-ice packs, vacuum sealing.
*   **Dispatch (The Promise):** Orders received before 2 PM are manifested and handed to AusPost/Toll Express within 12 hours.

## 3. Financial Mechanics
*   **Gross Margin:** 73% (After factoring in $500 batch tests and $15 thermal packaging COGS).
*   **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC):** ~$35. Relies entirely on word-of-mouth in Telegram and influencer affiliate seeding. Zero Meta/Google Ad spend.
*   **Average Order Value (AOV):** $250. Driven deliberately high by restricting single-vial sales and up-selling "Phase Protocols" (bundled stacks) and Reconstitution Kits.
*   **Payment Infrastructure:** Tier-1 Stripe integration layered with automated cryptocurrency (USDC/BTC) checkout rails to support anonymity preferences.

## 4. Scalability Bottlenecks
*   **The Freeze Constraint:** The raw volume of thermal packaging a single human operator can complete before the courier pickup cut-off. Scaling requires mirroring the freezer/dispatch setup (Nodes) across multiple states rather than centralizing a giant warehouse (which invites TGA/ABF physical raids).
