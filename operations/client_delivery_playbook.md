# Client Delivery & Fulfillment Playbook

**Company:** Peak Haven Australia
**Date:** 2026-03-06

---

## 1. Scope & Mandate
Unlike a service business, "Client Delivery" for Peak Haven means the physical manifestation of biological compounds from a decentralized freezer to a buyer's doorstep within a 48-hour thermal window. The margin of error is absolute zero.

*Refer to `operations/client_onboarding_system.md` for digital communications.*

## 2. Order Processing SLA (The 12-Hour Promise)

### Phase 1: Authentication & Triaging (9:00 AM)
1. **Payment Sweep:** Node Operators verify all Stripe, Bank Transfer, and Crypto payments manually. Suspicious/fraud-flagged orders are automatically cancelled and refunded to protect merchant status.
2. **Manifest Generation:** Filter ShipStation/Shopify by state destination. Allocate WA orders to Node West, East Coast orders to Node Prime.

### Phase 2: The Cold Extraction (11:00 AM)
1. **Sanitization:** Operators dawn nitrile gloves.
2. **Batch Matching:** Orders are picked against the specific Janoshik batch number listed on the packing slip. A mis-pick immediately breaches the 10x Guarantee.
3. **The Ice Run:** Vials are extracted from the -20°C deep freeze only when all thermal bags are staged and pre-cooled.

### Phase 3: Thermal Packaging (11:30 AM)
1. Each vial is placed into a light-proof Mylar seal.
2. Mylar seal goes into a 5mm polyurethane thermal bubble mailer.
3. 2x pharmaceutical-grade frozen gel packs are sandwiched around the Mylar pouch.
4. The outer thermal mailer is vacuum-sealed to prevent ambient heat transfer.

### Phase 4: Dispatch (2:00 PM)
1. Packages are driven to the central sorting facility (AusPost/Toll Express) directly before their 3:00 PM nationwide dispatch truck departs. 
2. **Crucial Rule:** We do not use localized street post boxes. They heat up to 60°C in the Australian sun and melt the ice packs instantly.

## 3. The Unboxing Experience
*The product must look like defense-contractor ordnance, not a supplement order.*

*   **Exterior:** Stark black mailer. Minimalist white shipping label.
*   **Interior:** The 10x Guarantee Card, printed on heavy stock. The Janoshik HPLC readout QR code is sticker-sealed over the Mylar pouch.
*   **The Message:** "If the seal is broken or the internal packs have reached ambient temperature, contact Inner Haven immediately via the encrypted link."

---

## 4. Exception Management (The Heat Policy)
*   **Courier Delay (Stuck in transit > 4 days):** Automatically flag order. Check ambient weather. If it sat in Queensland during a 40-degree heatwave, automatically reship a replacement *before* the customer complains. Claim transit insurance later. This action alone creates 10/10 Trustpilot reviews.
*   **Address Errors:** If returned to sender, the package is flagged as "Compromised." The vials are destroyed. They cannot be restocked due to the strict "No Returns" purity doctrine.
